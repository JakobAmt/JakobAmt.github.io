Amtedal Design & WatchMate

3D-printed watch stands, designed, manufactured, and sold direct

Product Design 3D Printing Fusion 360 Shopify Vipps API Entrepreneurship

Founded and operated a sole proprietorship selling 3D-printed watch stands designed and manufactured in-house, sold both online and in person at watch fairs across Norway.

Managed the full product lifecycle end to end: CAD design, 3D printing, modular packaging, e-commerce via Shopify, and direct sales at watch fairs. Also integrated the Vipps Payment API into a custom-built website for local checkout. aswell as media management on Instagram.
